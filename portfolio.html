This project is based on a common extension of a network by adding a hot-site for disaster recovery and redundant network services. This project included the installation of two Cisco ASR 1002-HX routers two Cisco Nexus 7009 switches, and two Cisco Nexus 5648 switches in support of the company headquarters and two branch offices.
